Game Rules

  • Your goal is to control the snake and eat as much food as possible to score points.
  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to control the direction of the snake.
  • The game starts with a single snake segment on the board. Move the snake around the board to collect food. Each time the snake eats food, it grows longer.
  • Avoid running into the walls or the snake's own body, as this will end the game. The game ends when the snake collides with a wall or itself.
  • Each time the snake eats food, your score increases. Additionally, there is a high score displayed, representing your best performance in the game.
  • When the game ends, a message will appear indicating "Game Over". Press any key to play again and restart the game.
  • Have fun playing the snake game and challenge yourself to beat your high score!
